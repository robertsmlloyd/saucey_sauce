Recipe Ideas using Saucey Sauce

Saucey Sauce Chicken  First, preheat oven to 400. Next, season chicken with your favorite seasonings. Then, cook chicken for 15-20 minutes. Finally, halfway through cooktime, cover chicken with your choice of Saucey Sauce.

Saucey Sauce Chutney  First, sauté lemongrass and ginger in a small pan. Next, add our high quality Tomato Saucey Sauce. Then, season with your choice of lime juice or cilantro. Finally, plate and serve with a helping of shrimp.

Saucey Sauce Grilled Wings  First, sauté garlic with your choice of a neutral oil in a small saucepan. Next, add our Original Saucey Sauce. Then, reduce and simmer the sauce for roughly twelve minutes. Finally, prepare your chicken to your liking to baste and serve.
Saucey Sauce Chicken Thighs  First, brown the chicekn thighs with a neatral oil and salt and pepper to your liking. Next, set aside the chicken for later use. Then, reduce and simmer the Garlic Saucey Sauce for roughly twenty minutes. Finally, baste the sauce and serve the chicken.

Saucey Sauce Inferno Wings  First, brine your wings in a combination of salt,sugar, thyme, and a few tablespoons of Spicey Saucey Sauce. Next, after several hours in the brine, parboil for ten minutes. Then, combine the secret combination of spices found on the label of the bottle. Finally, toss the chicken in the dry mix, serve, and enjoy.
