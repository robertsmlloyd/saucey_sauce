Saucey Sauce Chicken

  • First, preheat oven to 400.
  • Next, season chicken with your favorite seasonings.
  • Then, cook chicken for 15-20 minutes.
  • The most important step, halfway through cooktime, cover chicken with your choice of Saucey Sauce.
