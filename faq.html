Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Saucey Sauce's BBQ sauce spicey?  A: We do have one sauce (Spicey Saucey Sauce) which is definitely on the very very very spicy side. Our Garlic Saucey Sauce and Saucey Sauce BBQ are not spicy.

How do you make your BBQ sauces?  A: Only our valuedemployees are able to know the true secret to our sauces.

How can I work for Saucey Sauce?  A: We are more than happy to welcome new faces to Saucey Sauce! Please come into our store and ask one of our employees for an application. After the application is returned to us, we personally review it. If we feel as though you are a great fit for the company, you can start your brand new life with Saucey Sauce!
Why Indianola?  A: We chose Indianola for the small town feel, but it is close enough to Des Moines when we choose to expand.

Is Saucey Sauce involved with the Indianola Community?  A: Saucey Sauce is blessed to be in such a caring community and tries their hardest to be involved however they can. We have become sponsors for Indianola High School and Simpson College. We have helped cater events in the past such as the County Fair, sporting events, and the Hot Air Balloon Festival.

If you have any thoughts, questions, or concerns for Saucey Sauce, please do not hesitate to contact us. We love to hear how we can make our company better!
